### B. Dedup activity 7 ngày
```sql
SELECT date_trunc('day', crawled_at) AS day,
  count(*) FILTER (WHERE is_duplicate_of IS NULL) AS winners,
  count(*) FILTER (WHERE is_duplicate_of IS NOT NULL) AS dups,
  round(100.0 * count(*) FILTER (WHERE is_duplicate_of IS NOT NULL) / nullif(count(*), 0), 1) AS dup_pct
FROM electricity_news
WHERE crawled_at > now() - interval '7 days'
GROUP BY 1 ORDER BY 1 DESC;
```
